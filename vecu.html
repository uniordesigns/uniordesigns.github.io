vecu.net

vecu.net was my high school senior project. i worked on this fictional brand from the beginning of the school year to the last month of it.

role

brand identity and webpage ui

date

september 2020 - may 2021

client

fictional brand vecu

fictional brand vecu.net is specialized in selling clothes online. they needed me to find a way how to make this brand more recognizable by high school students. since all of us spend at least 2 hours a day on social media apps i decided that we need to work more on how to make things advertise themselves. i figured out that the easiest way to do that is by microinfluencering. a happy and satisfied customer will most of the time brag about the service on social media apps so we need to stand out by simplicity. a nice gesture would be thank you letter which i suggested and designed.

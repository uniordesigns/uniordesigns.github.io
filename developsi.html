developsi

developsi is a project started by a group of 5 high school friends. they asked me to help them establish their brand look and design an app that they are building at the moment.

role

brand identity and mobile app ui

date

february 2021 - present

client

developsi

developsi is building an app that will help bring tourism back to the cities in Slovenia after the disastrous year 2020. they want a modern and elegant look yet simple to use. since the app will be used by a lot of foreign tourists we want it to be that simple that they can use icons to navigate through it and ditch almost all text.

i was also asked to design their brand image. it was quite a challenge since i was doing it for the first time for a software company. they wanted something modern but not too complicated so it can be easily recognizable.
